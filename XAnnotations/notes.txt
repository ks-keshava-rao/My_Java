2. built in Annotations into two types :- Applied to the code(affect on code) and Applied to other Annotations .
3. inbuild Annotations

    4. @SuppressWarnings
    5. @SafeVarargs
4. Userdefined annotation can b added to class , methods , data members , variable etc.

    3. Target 
    4. Inherited 
    5. Repeatable
6. These all are usefull upon the userdefined annotation.
